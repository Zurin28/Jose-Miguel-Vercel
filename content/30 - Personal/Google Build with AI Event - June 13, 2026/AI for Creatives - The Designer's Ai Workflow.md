# 🎯 Ai doesn't replace designers, it replaces designers who doesn't use Ai.

- Creative work isn't always Easy.

## 💡 Where AI Helps
- **Idea generation**
- **Faster Exploration**
- **Faster Workflow**
- Layout adaptation

## ❓ What still needs humans
- **Creativity**
- **Storytelling**
- Emotions
- **Strategy**

- It is not creatives vs Ai, it is how you use AI

## ✅ Workflow as a designer
- Content Brief (Understand it well)
- Initial Design (Draft in Creative tool)
- **AI Visual Generation** (Drop in Chatgpt)
- **Design Refinement** 
- Final 
- Design
