# 📖 Genesis 39:10

- The story of Joseph on how he was tempted by Potiphar's Wife.
- Romans 8: Predestination (God has a plan for everyone.)
- Temptation always atacks us, resist the devil and he will flee against thee. The gates of hell shall not prevail we should fight it even if it hard.

## ⚠️ 4 THINGS TO FIGHT TEMPTATION

### 🎯 1 The Preparation (Be prepared for the Temptation)s

- We need to be prepared for the temptation.
- We already know **Satan** is going to tempt us, to be ready.

#### 💡 A. The Awareness of God

- Being aware that God is always there.
- Not hiding, like **Adam and Eve**. Yet, we are still nakes to God, we can't hide anything.
- We should be afraid that **God is always watching us**, and we should not sin.

#### 👣 B. Walk with God

- All that God wants, we do.

### ✅ 2 The Response (The response to the temptation)

- Dapat meron tayong paninindigan.
- If you know it is temptation, **flee from it**.

### 🔍 3 Learn the Nature and Course of Temptation

**Nature of Temptation**:

- 1 Appeal to our desire.
    - Kung saan natin gusto.
- 2 Appeal to our fear
    - Makes you fear of **missing out** and being **different from the world**.
    - To overcome fear, divert your focus to the God.
    - We are not busy for God, that is why we are busy in the world.
    - We should be **busy with God**.

### 💡 4 The Benefit of Temptation

- 1 It reveals our **true nature and Identity**.
- 2 Being Faithful in temptation leads to **blessing**.
- God works everything bad for good.
