## 💡 Opportunities Gained

### ✅ 1 Instant Provision

- We don't need to worry about anything, God will **provide**.
- Even when we can't do something, God will provide and **equip** us.
- We should not hesitate to **sacrifice** for God..
- If you think something is **impossible** for you, remember God turned water into Wine.
- We are the water, and God can **turn us into wine**.

### ❓ 2 Imputed Value

- We are **sinful** and we are **dead**, while we live in the flesh.
- But when we accept Christ we become **born again**, and God gives us **value**, we gain eternal life in heaven.
- It is because of God that **life has value**.
- What is the **meaning of living** if there i no life after?
- We should use the **bitter situation** as an inspiration, to continue and to gain value.

### 🔥 3 Increase our Favor

- We don't need people's favor, but **God's favor**.
- We have the favor from God, if we are **faithful**.
- God will give His favor to His **faithful servant**.
